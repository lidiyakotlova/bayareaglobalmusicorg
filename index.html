Competition Categories

Solo Performance

  • Keyboard (Piano, Organ, Harpsichord)

  • Woodwinds & Brass
  • Vocal (Classical & Contemporary)

Ensemble & Composition

  • Piano Duet / Duo

  • Original Composition
  • Traditional & Ethnic Instruments

Divisions & Fees

Division Age Group Time Limit Fee
Prodigy / Rising Star I Age 6 & Under 4 Minutes $65

Professional / Adult Ages 19+ 15 Minutes $100

Submission Requirements

  • Video: Unedited YouTube or Vimeo link. Must show face, hands, and instrument clearly.
  • Repertoire: One piece or one movement of a major work (Sonata/Concerto).
  • Memorization: Mandatory for all solo categories (optional for ensemble/composition).
  • Scores: PDF copies of music must be emailed to the organization within 3 days of application.
